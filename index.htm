  • How to start: Enter your name to registration on the beside text box, And then click the play button to start.
  • After start the game, To control your helicopter, Use your keyboard arrow keys, '←' to slow the speed, '→' to speed up, '↑' to go up ward, '↓' go down ward, respectively.
  • When you will here, danger beep mean will come a aeroplane from the opposite side of your helicopter, Always must to make appropriate distance. [Control tips: When your helicopter and the aeroplane will come near you should to decrease your speed by using on your keyboard '←'].
  • Always must to care the green pipes, Always make appropriate distance with the green pipes otherwise if do touch with green pipes then you will loose your control and your helicopter will be crash.
  • Some time will come rain with storm, There are nothing to panic.
  • Every 2 seconds after your score will be increase by 2.
  • If you did collide then don't worried about it, You will get a next chance, Over there will come a pop-up window just click on the play again button and you game will be start again.
